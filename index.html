About

Welcome to our Garry's Mod Freebuild Sandbox server.

We are still in the process of getting things set up. Frequent server restarts may occur. Please bear with us during this time.

If you encounter any issues, please be sure to give our friendly admins a heads up.

While you are in the process of loading, we strongly recommend that you subscribe to our Steam Workshop content collection that compliments this server — If you haven't done so already.

On a high bandwidth connection, please allow up to 2‐4 minutes before you can play. If this takes longer, we strongly advise that you pause all other ongoing downloads, including video streams.

Server Rules

We don't ask for much from you, but we do kindly ask that you:-

  • Don't be a dick.
  • Don't cry wolf.

Please be aware that our server also has ongoing active protection from the most common methods of griefing.

  • You will automatically be kicked for prop spam.
  • You can not constrain entities to the world.
  • You can not destroy what would otherwise normally be breakable entities.

Admins and moderators generally WILL NOT intervene to enforce the rules except for in extreme cases or unless requested to do so.-
